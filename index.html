About Me

I am a dedicated teacher based in Kakuma Refugee Camp, committed to empowering children through the art of storytelling. My mission is to help young learners discover their voices, build confidence, and use their stories to inspire positive change within their families and communities.

My Work

I work with children and youth to identify their talents, strengthen their literacy skills, and express themselves creatively. Through storytelling sessions, art programs, and mentorship, I help transform lives one story at a time.
